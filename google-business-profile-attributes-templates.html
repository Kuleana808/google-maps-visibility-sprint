Decision rule

How to choose the right attribute mix

Use attributes when they reduce one of four buyer frictions:
1. "Can this business help someone like me?"
2. "Do I need to book first or can I just call?"
3. "Do they come to me or do I go there?"
4. "Does this business feel trustworthy, accessible, and easy to work with?"

If an attribute does not reduce one of those frictions, do not force it on. Clean, accurate profiles beat stuffed profiles.
