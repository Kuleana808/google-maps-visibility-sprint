Free Google review response template library

Use these as starting points, not legal scripts or canned corporate replies. The goal is to respond quickly, sound human, acknowledge specifics, and move the next step offline when the issue needs real resolution.

Universal positive review

Short thank-you response

Thank you, [First Name] — we really appreciate you taking the time to share this. We are glad we could help with [service/job type], and we are grateful you chose [Business Name]. If you ever need anything again, we are here to help.
  • Best for 5-star reviews with short praise.
  • Repeat the service type when it sounds natural so future buyers understand what you do.
Universal detailed praise

Specific customer-win response

Thank you, [First Name]. We are glad to hear the [repair/treatment/project] felt smooth from start to finish. Our team works hard to make [specific outcome] easier for customers, so we appreciate you calling that out. Thanks again for trusting [Business Name].
  • Use when the reviewer mentions speed, professionalism, cleanliness, bedside manner, or a concrete outcome.
  • Keeps the response useful for future buyers reading the thread.
3-star / mixed feedback

Measured improvement response

Thank you for the feedback, [First Name]. We appreciate you highlighting both what went well and where we could have done better. We are reviewing your note with the team so we can improve the experience around [issue mentioned]. If you are open to it, please contact us at [phone/email] so we can make this right directly.
1-star or 2-star complaint

Calm public recovery response

We are sorry to hear this, [First Name]. This is not the experience we want for anyone working with [Business Name]. We would like to look into what happened and try to make it right. Please contact us at [phone/email] and ask for [manager/owner name] so we can review the details with you directly.
Plumbers / emergency jobs

Plumber review response

Thank you, [First Name]. We are glad we could help with your [leak/drain/water-heater] issue and get things handled quickly. Plumbing problems are stressful, so we appreciate you trusting [Business Name] to take care of it. If you ever need us again, we are just a call away.
HVAC / repair + install

HVAC review response

Thank you, [First Name]. We are happy to hear the [AC repair/heating service/install] went well and that our team made the process easier. We appreciate you choosing [Business Name] and taking the time to share your experience with other local homeowners.
Dentists / trust + comfort

Dental review response

Thank you, [First Name]. We know dental visits can feel stressful, so we really appreciate your trust in our team. We are glad your [cleaning/emergency visit/consult] felt comfortable and clear, and we are grateful you shared your experience with other local patients.
Med spas / consult quality

Med spa review response

Thank you, [First Name]. We are so glad you felt cared for during your visit with [Business Name]. Our goal is to make every [consult/treatment/follow-up] feel personal, clear, and comfortable, so we appreciate you taking the time to share this with others.
Roofers / estimates + projects

Roofing review response

Thank you, [First Name]. We appreciate the opportunity to help with your roofing project and are glad the communication and workmanship stood out. Thank you for trusting [Business Name] with your home and for sharing your experience with other local homeowners.
Tree service / cleanup + safety

Tree service review response

Thank you, [First Name]. We are glad we could help with your [tree removal/trimming/storm cleanup] and leave the property in good shape afterward. Safety and cleanup matter a lot in this work, so we appreciate you calling that out and sharing your experience.

How should a buyer use these templates?

Reply quickly, reference the real service or concern, and keep the tone calm. Positive reviews should reinforce trust. Negative reviews should acknowledge the issue, avoid arguing in public, and move resolution to phone or email when needed.
