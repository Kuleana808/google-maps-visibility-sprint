Free Google Business Profile post prompt library

Use these as starting prompts, not polished ad copy. The job is to make posting easier, highlight real services or results, and steadily strengthen trust signals around the work buyers already search for on Google Maps.

Universal weekly post

Service spotlight prompt

Write a short Google Business Profile post for [Business Name] promoting [core service]. Mention the problem it solves, one trust-building proof point, and a clear local call-to-action for [City]. Keep it simple, specific, and under 120 words.
  • Best for businesses that need a reusable weekly post format.
  • Swap the service name and city each week to keep the profile fresh.
Proof / review angle

Customer-result prompt

Write a Google Business Profile update for [Business Name] highlighting a recent customer win. Mention the service performed, the before/after result, and one sentence that builds trust without sounding hypey. End with an invitation to call or book from Google Maps.
  • Works well after a successful job, treatment, or estimate.
  • Pairs naturally with the review template library.
