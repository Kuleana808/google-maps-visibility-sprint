Free review request template library

Use the universal scripts first. Then swap in the niche versions when the customer journey, job type, or booking language matters. The goal is not fancy copy — it is making the review ask easy enough to send consistently.

Universal SMS

Short text message

Hi [First Name] — thanks again for choosing [Business Name]. If the job went well, would you mind leaving us a quick Google review here?

[Google Review Link]

It really helps more local customers find us. Thank you.
  • Best after a completed service call, pickup, or treatment.
  • Send within 2–24 hours while the outcome is still fresh.
Universal email

Short email follow-up

Subject: Quick favor from [Business Name]

Thank you again for working with [Business Name]. If you had a good experience, would you be open to leaving us a quick Google review?

Review link: [Google Review Link]

[Name]
[Business Name]
