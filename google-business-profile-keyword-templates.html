Free Google Business Profile keyword template library

Use these as phrase starters, not a stuffing checklist. The job is to make the profile more specific, more local, and more buyer-relevant while keeping the wording readable and trustworthy.

Universal keyword workflow

Core GBP keyword formula

[Primary service] + in [City/Area] + [top buyer outcome].

Examples:
- Emergency plumber in Austin for leak repair and same-day service.
- Invisalign dentist in Scottsdale for consult-driven cosmetic cases.
- House cleaning in Tampa for recurring weekly and move-out service.

Rule: use natural phrases a real buyer might search for, then place them where they fit cleanly inside the description, services, categories, posts, and offers.
  • Best for businesses that need a simple base pattern before going niche-specific.
  • Use this when the profile sounds generic, not when the copy is already overloaded.
Natural phrasing guardrail

How to avoid keyword stuffing

Use one primary phrase, one location phrase, and one outcome phrase per section.

Good:
We help Phoenix homeowners with AC repair, tune-ups, and same-week cooling service.

Bad:
Phoenix AC repair Phoenix HVAC service Phoenix air conditioning near me best Phoenix HVAC company.

If the line sounds robotic out loud, simplify it before publishing.
  • Useful when the buyer is worried about “SEO keywords” but needs a safe quality bar.
  • Pairs well with the optimization checklist when multiple fields feel messy.

Decision rule

How to choose the right GBP keywords

Start with the service buyers actually ask for.
Add the city or service area only where it sounds natural.
Add one outcome cue such as same-day, emergency, consultation, estimate, or recurring service.
Then spread the phrasing across the profile instead of repeating the same line everywhere.

If the wording only serves SEO and not the buyer, rewrite it.

How should a buyer use these templates?

Pick the closest niche starter, swap in the real city, services, and buyer outcome, then place the phrasing inside the description, services, products, offers, and posts where it reads naturally. The goal is not maximum repetition. The goal is a clearer local profile.
